Impact of Technology on Human Behavior

URL Alliant Article
Author Alliant International University
Abstract This article explains how technology affects human behavior and mental health. It discusses how social media, smartphones, and digital communication influence how people interact. While technology allows people to stay connected and access resources, it can also lead to problems such as anxiety, distraction, and social isolation. The article shows that psychologists study these effects to better understand how technology impacts mental health.

URL Simply Psychology
Author Simply Psychology
Abstract This source explains how neuroimaging technology helps psychologists study the brain and behavior. Tools such as MRI and CT scans allow researchers to observe brain activity and understand how different areas of the brain relate to thoughts and emotions. This technology has improved the understanding of mental disorders and helps psychologists develop better treatments. It shows how technology plays a major role in understanding human behavior.
