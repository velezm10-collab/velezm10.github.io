Artificial Intelligence in Psychology

URL APA AI Article
Author American Psychological Association Services
Abstract This source explains how artificial intelligence is used in psychology to improve diagnosis and treatment. AI can analyze large amounts of data such as behavior, speech, and medical history to detect mental health conditions early. It also helps psychologists by handling tasks like scheduling and record keeping, which allows them to focus more on patient care. The article emphasizes that AI should not replace human psychologists but should be used as a tool to support them. Overall, this source shows how AI is becoming an important part of modern psychological practice.
